About Me

I'm a full stack developer from Richmond, VA, and a graduate of the University of Richmond Coding Boot Camp. I've also studied culinary arts and music, which share one common characteristic... I love to create things. I took my love for creation and applied it to software development, so now I can share my creations in a different format.

For the past twelve years, I have been in the restaurant industry, with the last 6 of those years in management. The further I climbed up this ladder, the more I felt something was missing. I knew it was time to make the leap.

Now, I am a Teaching Assistant for the Coding Boot Camp at University of Richmond, as well as a Freelance Web Developer. I am looking for a great company to begin my journey as a full time Software Developer.

The technologies I have worked with are HTML, CSS, JavaScript/jQuery, AWS, Java, C#, Ruby, Node, Express, MySQL/Sequelize, MongoDB/Mongoose, Boostrap, Materialize, UI Kit, React/Redux, IndexedDB, PWA's, Socket.io, Node, Git, and RESTful API's.

My journey into web development has been, and still is, one of the most rewarding things I've ever done. I am constantly being challenged, and it is the challenge that keeps me motivated, inspired, and coming back for more.

Lorenzo's Listclose

Lorenzo's List is a heroku deployed web application where a user can create an account to view and post items to sell, as well as message other users in order to contact them about their posted items. The web pages in this application are dynamically created from data pulled from the database, and then rendered with handlebars.

On this project, I did all of the front end. The technologies we used to build this application was HTML, CSS, JavaScript/jQuery, Handlebars, Bootstrap, UI Kit, MySQL/Sequelize, Express, and a few external API's.
