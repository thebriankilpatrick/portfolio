About Brian Kilpatrick

I'm a studying full stack developer from Richmond, VA. I've also studied culinary arts and music, which share one common characteristic... I love to create things. I took my love for creation and applied it to web development, so now I can share my creations with you.


For the past twelve years, I have been in the restaurant industry, with the last 6 of those years in management. The further I climbed up this ladder, the more I felt something was missing. I knew it was time to make the leap.


I have worked with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery and other JS libraries, Node, various API's, and frameworks such as Bootstrap and Materialize.


My journey into web development has been, and still is, one of the most rewarding things I've ever done. I am constantly being challenged, and it is the challenge that keeps me motivated, inspired, and coming back for more.
